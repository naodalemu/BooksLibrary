Simple Rules

Don't take a book that you're not gonna use

Books are common tools of the society and since the books at our school are lent for free it's easy to take that service for granted. Please as soon as the book is useless to you return it back to the library.

No writing on the books

This is a common rule for a library. When taking a book responsibilities come with it. Use notebooks to memorize anything you want from the book. Not writing on the books is one responsibility.

Return books before your time is up

Books can be lent for a single person at once only for 7 days and if the student doesn't return the book before the time is up he/she will have to pay a fee based on how many days have passed since the 7 day deadline.
